Payroll Services


Internal payroll management can be time consuming, costly and requires adhering to strict deadlines together with being up to date with current legislation and regulations. At Accounting Solutions Limited, we have the knowledge, experience and expertise to ensure your payroll is accurately managed, prepared on time, meet the respective deadlines and comply with legislation and regulations.



Our Services include but are not limited to:

Calculation of NIS, PAYE, Health Surcharge

Preparation of Payroll Reports

Direct Deposits to Employees' Accounts

Preparation of NIS and BIR application forms and remitting payments to relevant statutory boards.

Preparation of TD1 Forms

Application of TD1 approvals

Preparation and Submission of TD4s and TD4 Summary Sheets to the Board of Inland Revenue (BIR)

Application of Company's NIS Number

Application of Employees' NIS Number

Assisting with Payroll Audits

Obtaining NIS Clearance

Liasing with relevant authorities with regards to any queries
