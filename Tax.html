Taxation Services

At Accounting Solutions Limited, we assist companies and individuals ( residents and non residents ) with their tax compliance and planning. We meet with our clients regularly to discuss tax planning and compliance in order to minimise tax liabilities, interest and penalties. Our services are as follows :


(i) Value Added Tax ( VAT ) :

  • VAT Registration

  • Computation and Competition of VAT Returns

  • Remittance Payments to the Board of Inland Revenue

  • Manage VAT Audits

  • Handle VAT Queries

(ii) Taxation :

  • Obtaining Company's BIR Number

  • Obtaining Company's BIR Number

  • Preparation and Submission of Corporate Tax Returns

  • Preparation and Submission of Personal Income Tax Returns

  • Obtaining VAT and Tax Clearances

  • Computation / Verification on Corporate Tax Liability

  • Computation of Business Levy, Green Fund Levy, Quarterly Tax and other Taxes.
