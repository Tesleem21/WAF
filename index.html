WAF is a global network of professional firms providing Audit, Tax and Advisory services.

Our work spans five areas: we audit many of the Nigeria's largest companies. And our people in Consulting, Deal Advisory, Tax & Law and Technology & Engineering work shoulder-to-shoulder with clients, ranging from small start-ups and individuals to major multinationals, in virtually every industry imaginable.

Our work is often complex, yet our mission is simple: to support the Nigeria in a connected world. It guides everything we do, underpinned by Our Values: Courage, Integrity, Excellence, Together and For Better. We use the power of our voice in Nigeria business to spark debate, share ideas and bring about change, using our global network, to draw on expertise from beyond the Nigeria borders.
